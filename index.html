HIGH-IMPRESS

high-impress

Presentations with high level impress.js

impress.js is a great tool for building impressive presentations in HTML5 & CSS3, and it is also open source.

But you a working on a fairly low level and it's hard for beginners to use. So high-impress steps in and trys to gives you a higher level abstraction based on the new HTML5 technologies.

At the moment it just adds nice custom HTML5 elements to build basic presentations, but more features like auto-layouts and plugins are on there way.

Features

  • 3D Transitions
  • Substeps
  • Background graphics
  • more ...
 # Titel A slide in written in __markdown__. * just import the plugin with <link rel="import" href="plugins/hi-markdown.html"> * annotate a step with the mdText="true" attribute * design simple slide without effort in markdown

Also in 3D
